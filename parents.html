Parent Resources & Safety Guide

Safety Features for All Users

At Roblox, we prioritize safety and civility across our platform. Here's how we protect our community:

For Users Under 13

  • Restricted access to certain features like voice chat
  • Additional content restrictions by default
  • Parental controls for managing account settings
  • Moderated communication systems

For Users 13+

  • Age-appropriate content access
  • Optional age verification
  • Customizable privacy settings
  • Well-being tools and screen time management

Parental Controls

We provide comprehensive tools to help you manage your child's experience:

  • Account Linking: Connect your account to monitor and manage your child's settings
  • Privacy Settings: Control who can interact with your child
  • Spending Limits: Set monthly spending caps on in-game purchases
  • Activity Reports: Receive regular updates on your child's account activity
